Inova wants to seize the existing resources in evolving markets,

and develop frugal solutions through inspiring innovation camps.

At Inova, we are a diverse team working together with Save the Children to arrange innovation camps.
These valuable camps will be the foundation and inspiration to further develop necessary solutions.

We believe that the best innovations come when interdisciplinary groups work together in a reliable and creative environment.
The camps are made to either solve a specific problem or create new ideas. We will base the camps on problems on both a local and global scale.

There is no limit to how big the problem or challenge can be.

Who and where?

We arrange innovation camps for

  • Schools
  • Businesses
  • Organizations
  • Local communities
The camps will be facilitated in a suitable place according to the number of attendees.
